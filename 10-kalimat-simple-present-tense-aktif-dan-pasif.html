12. buatlah 5 kalimat aktif dan pasif simple present tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Passive Voice

1. Active voice : He does his homework every night

   Passive voice : His homework is done by him every night

2. Active voice : She buys a dress for her daughter

    Passive voice : A new dress is bought by her for her daughter  

3.  Active voice  : My father drinks a cup of coffee every morning

     Passive voice : A cup of coffee is drunk by my father every morning

4. Active voice : Ameera brings her dictionary every Tuesday

   Passive voice : Her dictionary is brought by Ameera every Tuesday

5.  Active voice : Ammar feeds his pet every morning

    Passive voice : His pet is fed by Ammar every morning.

PEMBAHASAN:

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Kalimat aktif yang bisa dirubah menjadi kalimat pasif berbentuk kalimat aktif yang mengandung transitive verbs (kata kerja yang memerlukan objek langsung) seperti make, bring, buy, write dan sebagainya. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif yang mengandung intransitive verbs (kata kerja yang tidak memerlukan objek langsung) seperti cry, swim, go, arrive, dan die tidak bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif. Tetapi, apabila intransitive verbs tersebut diikuti oleh preposition, ada kemungkinan kalimat tersebut bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif tetapi kalimat pasif yang terbentuk akan sangat kaku.

Rumus Kalimat Pasif (Passive Voice)

Subject (object dari kalimat aktif) + to be ( disesuaikan dengan bentuk tensesnya + Verb 3 (past participle)

TENSES                                                 RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE

Simple present                                   subject + is/am/are + Verb 3

Present continuous                      subject + is/am/are + being + Verb 3

Present perfect                               subject + have/has been + Verb 3

Simple past                                       subject + was/were + Verb 3

Past continuous                         subject + was/were + being + Verb 3

Past perfect                                      subject + had been + Verb 3

Simple future                                     subject + will be + Verb 3

Be going to                         subject + is/am/are + going to + be + Verb 3

Future continuous                subject + will + be + being + Verb 3

Future perfect                                   subject + will have been + Verb 3

Modal                                              Subject + modal + be + Verb 3

Langkah-langkah (tips) membentuk kalimat pasif (Passive Voice) dari kalimat aktif:

Pilih objek penderita dari kalimat aktif itu Buatlah objek tersebut menjadi subjek dari kalimat pasifnya Perhatiakan bentuk tensesnya, subjeknya tunggal atau jamak karena tenses yang di gunakan harus sama Bentuklah predikatnya dengan to be + Verb 3

15. Pasif aktif 1.simple present tense2. Present future tense3. Present perpect tense4. Present continius tense5.simple past tense6.past continius tense


Pasif aktif 
1. simple present tense
Aktif : Subject + v1
Pasif : Obejct + am/is/are + v3 + by subject

Contoh :
She writes a letter every day
A letter is written every day by her.

2. Present future tense
Aktif : subject + will + v1
Pasif : object + will be + v3 + by subject

Contoh :
She will give a book tomorrow
A book will be given tomorrow by her.

3. Present perfect tense
Aktif : subject + have/has + v3 
Pasif : object + have/has + been + v3 + by subject

Contoh :
He has watched that movie for 2 hours.
That movie has been watched for 2 hours by him

4. Present continuous tense
Aktif : Subject + am/is/are + v-ing
Pasif : object + am/is/are + being +v3 + by subject

Contoh :
They are playing football now.
Football is being played now by them.

5.simple past tense
aktif : subject = v2
Pasif : object + was/were + v3 + by subject

Contoh :
we cooked dinner last night
Dinner was cooked last night by us.

6. past continuous tense
Aktif : subject + was/were + v-ing
Pasif : object + was/were + being + v3 + by subject

Contoh :
They were singing a song when I came.
A song was being sung by them when I came.

18. jadi tugasnya ada 3 kalimat aktif, yaitu 1 kalimat simple present tense, 1 kalimat simple past tense, 1 kalimat simple future, lalu rubah menjadi pasif?​


Simple Present Tense

Membicarakan aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung atau kebiasaan

Active voice:

S + V1 + O

Passive voice:

S + to be (is, am, are) + V3

Contoh kalimat:

Active = I eat cake

Passive = Cake is eaten by me

Simple Past Tense

Membicarakan aktivitas di masa lalu

Active voice:

S + V2 + O

Passive voice:

S + to be (was, were) + V3

Contoh kalimat:

She wrote a letter yesterday

A letter is written by her yesterday

Simple Future Tense

Membicarakan aktivitas di masa depan atau mendatang

Active voice:

S + will + V1 atau S + to be (is, am, are) + going to + V1

Passive voice:

S + will be + V3 atau S + to be (is, am, are) + going to be + V3

Contoh kalimat:

They will meet me

I will be met by them

Berikut ini adalah 5 contoh kalinat aktif yang diubah menjadi kalimat pasif masing-masing untuk Simple Present Tense dan Simple Past Tense.

JAWABAN

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE:

1.

Aktif: They like your shoes.

Pasif: Your shoes are liked by them.

2.

Aktif: I want an iPhone.

Pasif: An iPhone is wanted by me.

3.

Aktif: She hates my dog.

Pasif: My dog is hated by her.

4.

Pasif: I am loved by him.

5.

Aktif: They fix bicycles.

Pasif: Bicycles are fixed by them.

SIMPLE PAST TENSE:

1.

Aktif: He washed my motorcycle.

Pasif: My motorcycle was washed by him.

2.

Aktif: I loved cats.

Pasif: Cats were loved by me.

3.

Aktif: We played basketball.

Pasif: Basketball was played by us.

4.

Aktif: She cooked breakfast this morning.

Pasif: Breakfast was cooked by her this morning.

5.

Aktif: My mother did the dishes yesterday.

Pasif: The dishes were done by my mother yesterday.

___________

PENJELASAN

Rumus kalimat pasif Simple Present Tense:

Subject + is/am/are + Verb 3 + by + Agent

Rumus kalimat pasif Simple Past Tense:

Subject + was/were + Verb 3 + by + Agent

Catatan:

→ Bentuk is digunakan bila subjek dalam kalimat adalah: He, she, it, dan subjek tunggal.

→ Bentuk am khusus digunakan bila subjek dalam kalimat adalah: I.

→ Bentuk are digunakan bila subjek dalam kalimat adalah: You, we, they, dan subjek jamak.

→ Bentuk was digunakan bila subjek dalam kalimat adalah: I, he, she, it, dan subjek tunggal.

→ Bentuk were digunakan bila subjek dalam kalimat adalah: You, we, they, dan subjek jamak.

→ Contoh subjek jamak:

Cats

Shoes

Bicycles

Dishes, dll.

→ Contoh subjek tunggal:

Dog

Breakfast

Basketball

iPhone

Motorcycle, dll.
