13. Bahasa inggris kelas 8 semester 2 activity 13​


Jawaban:

1. Mother: I'll be at the store for a while. I want you to clean the house while I'm away. I'll be back as soon as possible.

Me: Okay, I'll clean the house right away Mom.

2. Me: I can't come to our Japanese course this afternoon. Would you please tell our tutor about my absence, please?

My Friend: Yes, I'd tell him later.

3. Me (Leader): Please forward this message to everyone regarding our working bee plan to clean the environment. Thank you!

Member: Yes, sir! Right away!

4. Me: I'm sorry that I can't come on time to join our English club activity because I have to help my sister do her homework first.

My friend: That's okay, don't worry about that! It's better late than never.

5. Teacher: We are going to have a virtual trip to a zoo tomorrow! I want everyone to prepare their laptop, smartphone and internet connection ready!

Students: Yes teacher!

Penjelasan:

Untuk nomor 1. pola kalimat mengikuti Simple Present Tense & Future tense karena menyatakan kegiatan di masa depan.

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object (Simple Present Tense)

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

Untuk nomor 2. pola kalimat memakai Modal Verb:

(-) Subject + can + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Would + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

Untuk nomor 3. Memakai pola kalimat Imperative:

Polite form: (+) Please, Verb-1 + Subject + Object

Untuk nomor 4. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Present Tense dan Modal Verb:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective (kata sifat)

Modal Verb: (+) Subject + can + Verb-1 + Object

Untuk nomor 5. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Future Tense dan Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + going to + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Semoga membantu ya.

Penjelasan:

Teks prosedur yang berhubungan dengan pertanyaan di atas adalah

PEPES IKAN

(Grilled marinated fish wrapped in Banana leaf)

1. Cut fish fillet into cubes.

2. Season fish fillet with salt, pepper, lime juice and yellow paste.

3. Place the marinated fish cubes on the banana leaf with sliced tomato, lemon basil, salam leaf, and kefir lime leaf on top. Fold into a parcel, securing the ends with toothpicks.

4. Grill over charcoal or sear in a frying pan for about 10 minutes on each side, until the leaf edges are getting charred.

5. Serve with steamed rice and vegetable.
