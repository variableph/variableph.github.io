16. 1.arrange the following words into a good sentence .the - orphanage - I - visit - will2.arrange the following words into a good sentence .Will not - I - because of - my influenza - the swimming pool - go to3.arrange the following words into a good sentence .stay - I - at home - if - it - Will - rains4.arrange the following words into a good sentence .the dinner - and - I - Chantika - Will - prepare5.arrange the following words into a good sentence .beef - we - Will - fry - not


Simple Future Tense

JAWABAN:

1. Arrange the following words into a good sentence .

the - orphanage - I - visit - will

=> I will visit the orphanage (Aku akan mengunjungi panti asuhan)

2. Arrange the following words into a good sentence .

Will not - I - because of - my influenza - the swimming pool - go to

=> I will not go to the swimming pool because of my influenza (Aku tidak akan pergi ke kolam renang karena influenza ku)

3. Arrange the following words into a good sentence .

stay - I - at home - if - it - Will - rains

=> I will stay at home if it rains. (Aku akan tinggal di rumah jika hujan)

4. Arrange the following words into a good sentence .

the dinner - and - I - Chantika - Will - prepare

=> I and Chantika will prepare the dinner (Aku dan Chantika akan menyiapkan makan malam)

5. Arrange the following words into a good sentence .

beef - we - Will - fry - not

=> We will not fry beef (Kami tidak akan menggoreng daging sapi)

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Future Tense

Simple Future tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi terjadi dimasa depan, secara spontan atau terencana.  

Keterangan waktu yang biasa digunakan adalah tomorrow, tonight, next week, next ... etc.  

Formula  

Verbal Sentence  

(+) Subject + shall/will + Verb I (infinitive)  (-) Subject + shall/will + not + Verb I (infinitive)  (?) Shall/will + Subject + Verb I (infinitive)  

Nominal sentence  

(+) Subject + shall/will + be + noun/adjective/adverb  (-) Subject + shall/will + be + not + noun/adjective/adverb  (?) Shall/will + Subject + be + noun/adjective/adverb?  

Contoh:  

1. (+) She will be my wife  

 (-) She will not be my wife  

 (?) Will she be my wife?  

2. (+) He will be here next month  

  (-) He will not be here next month  

  (?) Will he be here next month?  

3. (+) They will visit us next weekend  

  (-) They will not visit us next weekend  

  (?) Will they visit us next weekend?  

4. (+) She will drive to Bandung on next Sunday  

  (-) She will not drive to Bandung on next Sunday  

  (?) Will she drive to Bandung on next Sunday?  

5. (+) Amy will swim in the swimming pool  tomorrow

  (-) Amy will not  swim in the swimming pool  tomorrow

  (?) Will Amy swim in the swimming pool tomorrow?  

6. (+) You will go to bed at 9  

  (-) You will not go to bed at 9?

  (?) Will you go to bed at 9?  

7. (+) She will buy dresses in that supermarket.  

  (-) She will not often buy dresses in that supermarket.  

  (?) Will she buy dresses in that supermarket?  

8. (+) The shop will close late on next Sunday  

  (-)The shop will not close late on next Sunday

  (?) Will the shop close late on next Sunday?

9. (+) The floor is so dirty, I will clean it all day  

  (-) The floor is so dirty, I will not clean it all day  

  (?) The floor is so dirty, will you clean it all day?  

10. (+) The show will start at 9.00 tonight

  (-) The show will not start at 9.00 tonight

  (?) Will the show start at 9.00 tonight?
