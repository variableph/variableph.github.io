Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

a - b = 4

a = 4 + b

56 = 1/2 (4 + b + b) x 4

56 = 1/2 (4 + 2b) x 4

56 = (2 + b) x 4

56 = 8 + 4b

4b = 48

b = 48 : 4 = 12 cm

a = 4 + b = 4 + 12 = 16 cm
