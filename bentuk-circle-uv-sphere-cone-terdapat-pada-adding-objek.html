14. a cylinder and a cone of the same height 2r and the same base diameter 2r. A sphere has a diameter of 2r. Find the ratio of the volume of the cylinder to that of the cone and to that of the sphere


Jawab:

6:1:4

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Volume of the cylinder = [tex]\pi r^{2}h=\pi r^{2}(2r)=2\pi r^{3}[/tex]

Volume of the cone = [tex]\frac{1}{3} \pi r^{2}h=\frac{1}{3} \pi r^{2}(2r)=\frac{2}{3} \pi r^{3}[/tex] and,

Volume of the sphere = [tex]\frac{4}{3} \pi r^{3}[/tex]

thus, the ratio of the volume of the cylinder to that of the cone and to that of the sphere are

Volume of the cylinder : Volume of the cone : Volume of the sphere

[tex]2\pi r^{3}:\frac{2}{3} \pi r^{3}:\frac{4}{3} \pi r^{3}\\=2:\frac{1}{3} : \frac{4}{3} \\=6:1:4 \\[/tex]

16. apa perbedaan circle, sphere, and round?​


Jawaban:

Round” adalah bentuk bulat yang cenderung seperti bola, sementara “circle” atau “circular” lebih pipih / gepeng, meskipun bentuk dasar bulatnya mungkin sama.

- round
kata ini berarti ‘bundar’ (2 dimensi) atau ‘bulat’ (3 dimensi), dan oleh karena itu bisa diterapkan untuk benda-benda dua dimensi pipih maupun benda tiga dimensi. jadi, kata ini mempunyai makna paling luas dibanding 2 kata lainnya.

- circle
kata ini juga berarti ‘bulat’ (2 dimensi), dan oleh karena itu hanya bisa digunakan untuk obyek 2 dimensi pipih. jadi, dalam dua contoh kalimat pertama di atas, kata round bisa digantikan oleh circular.

- sphere
kata ini hanya bisa berarti ‘bulat’ (3 dimensi), dan oleh karena itu hanya bisa digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan benda-benda bulat tiga dimensi seperti planet bumi, kelereng, bola, anting-anting bula, alat peraga globe, dan lain-lain.

25. 1. The diagram shows a cone of base radius 5 cm and height 10 cm, and a sphere of radius 5 cm. Find the ratio of a. The volumes of the cone and the sphere, b. the curved surface areas of the cone and the sphere. ​


Jawaban:

√5/4.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

a. The volume of a cone is given by Vcone = (1/3)πr^2h, where r is the radius of the base and h is the height. The volume of a sphere is given by Vsphere = (4/3)πr^3, where r is the radius of the sphere.

Plugging in the values, we get:

Vcone = (1/3)π(5)^2(10) = 250/3π

Vsphere = (4/3)π(5)^3 = 500/3π

The ratio of the volumes is Vcone/Vsphere = (250/3π)/(500/3π) = 1/2.

b. The curved surface area of a cone is given by Lcone = πrℓ, where r is the radius of the base and ℓ is the slant height of the cone. The curved surface area of a sphere is given by Lsphere = 4πr^2, where r is the radius of the sphere.

To find the slant height of the cone, we use the Pythagorean theorem:

h^2 + r^2 = ℓ^2

10^2 + 5^2 = ℓ^2

ℓ = √125

Plugging in the values, we get:

Lcone = π(5)(√125) = 25π√5

Lsphere = 4π(5)^2 = 100π

The ratio of the curved surface areas is Lcone/Lsphere = (25π√5)/(100π) = √5/4.
