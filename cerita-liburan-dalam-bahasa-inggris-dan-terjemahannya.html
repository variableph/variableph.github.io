26th December has come, it's time for my school holiday. I've been waiting for this day to come. Well, my family and I haven't planned on going somewhere yet to spend our holiday. Hmm.. maybe not, we're not going somewhere this holiday, because I want to spend my holiday at home. I want to feel how's it feel to be at home when a long holiday comes. After I satisfied on playing games and watching TV, I looked at the clock, and I was surprised, "Huh?! 10 O'clock?! Wow that was quick! After I thanked my parents, I quickly set things up, and boot it up. Then I inserted a game disk, and started playing it (my parents also gave me 3 games, and those are my favoite games). (1 day later, 31st December) It's 23:59 now, It's time to prepare things up to celebrate New Year. I can wait to light up the fireworks to celebrate it. Oh God this is it.. 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.. Happy New Year!! *lighted up the fireworks*. "Wow, this is the best New Year celebration ever! Those fireworks are very beautiful! I can't forget this moment!". I also said a wish, " I wish I can be more successful, and I wish I can change to a better person this year. Thanks for everything 2016.." (4 days later) "Wow, 4th January already. Well this is good, because I can finally meet my friends again at school. Well, better prepare things up for school tomorrow.."
That's my story, hope you can understand it :)

New Year’s Eve celebration

On New Year’s Eve 2022, my big family and I celebrated New Year’s Eve together. We held a big dinner planned by my father and my uncle in my house. Before the celebration, we all prepared what was needed such as ingredients of food, fireworks, trumpet, and sound system.

The weather on the day was not so bright. Drizzling rain which flushed the earth since the morning did want to not stop. But it did not dampen our spirits. My cousin and I went to the market to buy fireworks. We bought quite a lot of fireworks on that day to enliven our big event. After getting fireworks, we helped my father and uncle to prepare karaoke appliance and sound system at home, while my mother and aunt prepared chicken meat and seasoning to be in roast tonight.

Precisely at 18.00 everything we need was ready. We were just waiting for 22.00 o’clock. We deliberately postponed the dinner time so that we could enjoy the food we made later. While we were waiting, I and my big family joked and laughed together in the living room. We also did karaoke together; each of us had to sing a song. My uncle who was good in singing did not want to miss to entertain us. At 22:00 we started to prepare the food. My father, uncle, cousin and I prepared the chicken meat to be grilled, while my mother, aunt and sister prepared rice, side dishes and drinks. After the chicken meat was cooked, we ate together while waiting at 12.00. We ate with gusto at that time and we fell so happy to eat together.

At 11:45 we started to prepare the fireworks to be burned. After the time was closed to 12.o’clock, we counted down to set off fireworks. We immediately set off fireworks right along with the turn of the year. We also blew the trumpet to add to the excitement of the evening. It’s amazing to see the fireworks show together with my big family. I hope we can hold this event again next year.

Pengalaman liburan di rumah termasuk dalam jenis recount text. Jadi, untuk menceritakan pengalaman liburan di rumah yang dialami oleh penulisnya menggunakan past tense.

Pembahasan

Contoh cerita pengalaman liburan di rumah dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris yaitu

         I spent my school holiday at home last year. I didn't go anywhere because of the rain. I spent my time in front of television or read books. My mother bought me some books. She said that it was better to read books than play game online.

         I usually woke up without alarm and took a bath when my mother asked me. After that, I played with my cat, watched television, or read a book. I also helped my mother, such as washed the dishes, swept the floor, or washed the clothes. It looked so bored. But I enjoyed my time very much. It was the best time of mine, because I hadn't to do my homework or study.

         Saya biasanya bangun tanpa alarm dan mandi ketika ibu menyuruh saya. Setelah itu, saya bermain dengan kucing saya, menonton TV, atau membaca buku. Saya juga membantu ibu saya, seperti mencuci peralatan makan, menyapu lantai, atau mencuci baju. Tampak sangat membosankan. Tetapi saya amat menikmati waktu saya. 18. contoh cerita liburan tahun baru dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Contoh cerita liburan tahun baru. Libur tahun baru merupakan hari libur Internasional dalam rangka merayakan pergantian tahun masehi. Untuk menceritakan liburan tahun baru yang telah berlalu, menggunakan tensis kalimat yang telah terjadi.

Pembahasan

                                          New Year Holiday Story

The last school holiday, I spent the time with my family on vacation to a relative's house in Madiun, approximately 6 hour trip by bus from our home in Malang.

On the way there, we saw many beautiful views such as rice fields, mountains, etc. Having arrived at the uncle's house, we were greeted by our relatives who had not met us for a long time. The next day, we walked around the town of Madiun until the sky got dark. Finally we decided to go home at 6:00 p.m. on that day.

On the next day, I forgot that it was the New Year. Sudenly, one of my cousins ​​came and asked me to celebrate the New Year's Eve together. At night, I went together with him to the center of the city of Madiun which had already been so crowded because a lot of people who also wanted to celebrate the New Year with his family. When the countdown arrived, the sky which was dark at once then became so colorfully decorated by fireworks. We prayed together and watched the fireworks lightening the sky that night.

Suddenly, the holiday passed quickly. The last New Year's holiday experience in the city of Malang was so impressive for me.

Terjemahan dalam Bahasa Indonesia

                                       Cerita Liburan Tahun Baru

Liburan sekolah yang lalu, aku menghabiskan waktu bersama keluarga dengan berlibur ke rumah saudara di Madiun, kurang lebih 6 jam perjalanan menggunakan angkutan bis dari rumah kami di Malang.

Dalam perjalanan kami melihat banyak pemandangan yang indah seperti sawah, gunung ,dan lain lain. Sesampainya di rumah paman, kami disambut oleh saudara-saudara kami yang sudah lama tak berjumpa dengan kami. Keesokan harinya, kami jalan-jalan mengelilingi kota Madiun hingga sore hari. Akhirnya kami memutuskan untuk pulang pada pukul 18.00 pada hari itu.

Di hari berikutnya, aku lupa bahwa hari itu adalah tepat di tahun baru. Tiba - tiba salah seorang sepupuku datang dan mengajak untuk merayakan malam pergantian tahun bersama. Di malam harinya aku pergi bersamanya ke pusat kota Madiun yang sudah ramai sekali karena banyak orang yang juga ingin merayakan tahun baru bersama keluarganya. Saat perhitungan mundur tiba, langit yang tadinya gelap sekarang menjadi warna-warni dihiasi oleh kembang api. Kami berdoa bersama sambil menyaksikan kembang api yang menerangi langit malam itu.

Tak terasa libur pun berlalu dengan cepat. Pengalaman libur tahun baru yang lalu di kota Malang sungguh mengesankan bagiku.
