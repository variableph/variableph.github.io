1. Joe used 2 piggy banks to save money – Piggy Bank A for toys and Piggy Bank B for books. The ratio of the money in Piggy Bank A to Piggy Bank B was 7 : 9. There was $612 in Piggy Bank B. Without removing any money from Piggy Bank B, how much money must Joe put into Piggy Bank A such that they have an equal amount of money in them?.


Direct proportion is a mathematical comparison between two numbers where the ratio of the two numbers is equal to a constant value. Joe must put $476 into Piggy Bank A so Piggy Bank A and Piggy Bank B have an equal amount of money.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah

Rumus

[tex]\frac{A}{B} = \frac{MA}{MB}[/tex]

Keterangan:

A = ratio Piggy Bank A

B = ratio Piggy Bank B

MA = money in Piggy Bank A

MB = money in Piggy Bank B

Diketahui :

Ratio of the money in Piggy Bank A to Piggy Bank B was 7 : 9There was $612 in Piggy Bank B

Ditanya : how much money must Joe put into Piggy Bank A?

Jawab :

Langkah 1 Memasukkan Nilai yang Telah Diketahui ke Dalam Rumus

Ratio Piggy Bank A to Piggy Bank B = 7 : 9 dapat ditulis dengan [tex]\frac{7}{9}[/tex].

[tex]\frac{A}{B} = \frac{MA}{MB}\\ \frac{7}{9} = \frac{MA}{612}[/tex]

Langkah 2 Menyelesaikan Operasi Hitung

[tex]\frac{A}{B} = \frac{MA}{MB}\\ \frac{7}{9} = \frac{MA}{612} \\MA = \frac{7}{9} . 612\\ MA = 476[/tex]

So, Joe must put $476 into Piggy Bank A to have an equal amount of money in Piggy Bank A and Piggy Bank B.

1. The soccer players cannot play in the stadium. It is being renovated.

2. We did not go to the conference. We weren't selected.

3. The money is saved in a piggy bank.

4. It is a good book. When was it first published?

5. Most European cars are manufactured in Germany,

6. Selfie sticks are one of the best inventions, When were they invented?

7. My car is washed in Clean & Shinny Auto Service twice a month.

------------------

EXPLANATION

Passive voice adalah suatu grammatical voice di mana subjek kalimat adalah penerima aksi bukan pelaku aksi seperti dalam active voice.

Konstruksi kalimat dalam passive voice adalah:

S + be + past participle (v₃)

Di mana "be" disesuaikan dengan tense kalimat yang digunakan:

present simple ⟹ is/am/are

present progressive ⟹ is/am/are + being

past simple ⟹ was/were

-------------------

EXPLICATION

1. Soccer players tidak dapat bermainnya sekarang, maka gunakan present progressive.

2. Tidak dapat perginya lamapu, maka tidak dipilihnya pun lampau (past simple).

3. Tidak ada keterangan waktu, gunakan saja present simple.

4. "publish" = terbit. Karena bukunya sudah diketahu bagus, pasti terbitnya dulu; gunakan past simple.

5. Bahwa mobil eropa diproduksi di Jerman masih berupa fakta sekarang; gunakan present simple.

6. Lihat penjelasan no 4.

7. "twice a month" (dua kali sebulan) menandakan kegiatan ini rutin dilakukan. Gunakan present simple.
