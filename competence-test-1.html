13. LKS B.Inggris Kelas 8 Competence Test 2


Jawaban:

LKS Bahasa Inggris kelas VIII Competence test 2 yaitu to hone and test your ability to think rationally, logically, and critically, try to choose one correct answer.

1. Whom is the message addressed to? Mila (a)

2. " ... will be canceled as the chief has to join the English speech contest."

The closest meaning of the word "canceled" is postponed (d)

3. Why was the meeting canceled? OSIS leader will join English speech contest (c)

4. Hidayah: Excuse me. Can you tell me where the library is? (b)

Dhimas: Sure. It's in front of the language laboratory.

Hidayah: Thanks.

5. Nabila: It's windy here. Can you close the door, please? (b)

Fahmi: Sure, Bil.

Penjelasan:

1. Whom is the message addressed to berarti kepada siapa pesan itu dialamatkan.

2. Canceled berarti dibatalkan. Sinonim canceled adalah postponed.

3. The chief yang dimaksud dalam pesan tersebut adalah OSIS leader.

4. Can you tell me where the library is berarti dapatkah kamu memberi tahu saya di manakah perpustakaan.

5. Windy berarti berangin. Sehingga close the door, bukan open the door.
