A: What happen to you? Why do you look confused?

B: Oh A, I have some problem about where I go after graduating from junior high school next. I'm confused because I don't know what I should choose between SMA or SMK. Could you give me your opinion about my problem?

A: Of course, B. In my opinion, it's not about talking among SMA and SMK, but it talks about what you need and want to do for next step. You can take SMA for getting wide knowledge, and You can take SMK for preparing your softskills in working world.

Asking and Giving Agreement

X: Hi, Z

Z: Hello, X

X: Z, I want to ask you something

Z: Okay X, what is your question about?

Z: Yes, I agree. As we can see that indonesia has many traditional culture from each province consisting their identity, one of the most famous culture is traditional dance such as Saman, Kecak, and etc

30. dialog asking anda giving opinion tema liburan


Kelas: IX
Mata pelajaran: Bahasa Inggris
Materi: Conversation
Kata kunci: advice, opinion

Pembahasan :

Dialog asking and giving opinion tema liburan:

Riri, Bayu, dan Santi berdialog membahas liburan mereka. Riri memberi advice (saran) untuk berkibur di pantai dan teman-temanya memberi pendapat (opinion) tentang saran tersebut.


Riri : Let's do something together this long weekend.
Santi: It's great idea ! What about going to the beach?
Bayu: Or we could watch movie in the cinema.
Riri : Going to beach sounds fun. We could get fresh air and play in the sands.
Santi: What movie arou you planning to watch, Bayu?
Bayu : Spider-Man. It is a superhero movie. It has great story and fight scenes.
Riri : We will have more fun playing on the beach than just watching movie.
Santi: I agree Riri.
Bayu: I would rather watch movie, really. I don’t like the heat and crowd on the beach.
Riri : We can swim or play in beach. Watching movie is so dull.
Santi: Besides, we can watch movie anyday. We can only go to beach during long vacation like this weekend.
Bayu: Fine, I will go with you then.
Riri : That’s nice to hear Bayu..
Santi: So, we are going to beach then. We will go to the beach together. We will have great a weekend there.
Bayu: Yes, I hope so.
