1. contoh kalimat congratulation dan responnya


We congratulate people when we hear good news about someone's achievement, promotion, graduation, etc. The social purpose of congratulating people is to express our happiness about someone's achievement.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat congratulation dan responsnya yaitu

1. “Congratulations on your wedding. I wish you a happy life ahead.”

Response: “It’s so nice of you to say so.”

2. “Please accept my warmest congratulations on your success.”

Response: “Thank you so much.”

3. “I’d like to congratulate you on the opening of your new store.”

Response: “Thank you, I really appreciate it.”

4. “Congratulations on your new business.”

Response: “Thank you.”

5. “Congratulations on your new position as the manager in your company.”

Response: “Thank you for your kind guidance.”

6. “Good job on your promotion!”

Response: "Thanks!"

7. “Congrats on your wedding.”

Response: “Thanks a lot.”

8. “I saw your performance on the dance show tonight. That’s so fantastic!”

Response: “Thanks! I’m so happy to hear that.”

9. “Congrats on your graduation! You did a good job.”

Response: “Thank you.”

10. “Good job!”

Response: “Thank you.”

28. Contoh kalimat congratulation tentang prestasi pendidikan


1. Congratulations for achieving the target! Selamat telah mencapai target!

2. Well done! You are destined to make it big, you just didn't know it. This recognition is surely deserved and will give you an opportunity to spread your wings. Bagus! Anda ditakdirkan untuk menjadi besar, Anda hanya tidak tahu itu. Pengakuan ini benar-benar layak dan akan memberi Anda kesempatan untuk melebarkan sayap Anda.

3. It's a pleasure to work with someone who knows how to make a friendly and inspiring work environment. I'm glad that a position opened up for you that is a great next step in your career. Adalah sebuah kebahagiaan untuk bekerja dengan seseorang yang tahu bagaimana membuat lingkungan kerja menjadi ramah dan inspiratif. Aku senang sebuah posisi dibuka untuk Anda yang merupakan langkah besar berikutnya dalam karir Anda.

4. Congratulations on your success! You have made us all proud. Keep up the good work! Selamat atas keberhasilan Anda! Anda telah membuat kami semua bangga. Tetaplah bekerja dengan baik!

5. If Oscars were given for a job well done, I'd nominate you! Congratulations for your fantastic achievement! Jika Oscar diberikan untuk pekerjaan yang dilakukan dengan baik, saya akan mencalonkan Anda! Selamat untuk prestasi fantastis Anda!

6. Your dedication, enthusiasm and insight are really inspiring. I wish you many years of great achievements! Dedikasi, semangat dan wawasan Anda benar-benar menginspirasi. Saya berharap semoga Anda mendapat prestasi besar bertahun-tahun!
