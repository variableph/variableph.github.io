1. Contoh kalimat present perfect continuous tense positif negatif dan tanya


Jawaban:

(+) she have been sleeping for 2 hours.

(-) she haven't been sleeping for 2 hours.

(?) have she been sleeping for 2 hours?

Present perfect tense dipakai untuk menyatakan perbuatan atau situasi yang dimulai di masa lampau dan sudah selesai atau masih terjadi atau masih ada hubungannya dengan masa sekarang.

Formula untuk positive:

Subject + has/have + Verb 3

Formula untuk negative:

Subject + has/have (not) + Verb 3

Formula untuk interrogative:

Has/have + subject + Verb 3 (?)

Has: She, he, it

Have: I, you, they, we

Contoh kalimat:

(+) I have eaten breakfast.

(-) I have not eaten breakfast.

(?) Have I eaten breakfast?

(+) She has done her homework.

(-) She has not done her homework.

(?) Has she done her homework?


2. Buatlah contoh kalimat present continuous tense (Kalimat positif,Kalimat negatif,Kalimat tanya)​


Kalimat (+): Ani is playing guitar

kalimat (-): Ani isn't playing guitar

kalimat (?): Is Ani playing guitar?

Present Continuous Tense

Positif  (+)

S + am/is/are + Verb (-ing)

ex : She is sleeping right now

We are studying today

Negatif  (-)

S + am/is/are + not + verb (-ing)

ex : She is not sleeping right now

We are not studying today

Interrogatif  (?)

am/is/are + S + Verb (-ing) + ?

ex : Is she sleeping right now?

Are we studying today?

S + to be (is,am,are) + V-ing + O

Formula Negative Sentence:

S + to be (is,am,are) + non + V-ing + O

To be (is,am,are) + S + V-ing + O?

She is reading a novel bookShe is not reading a novel bookIs she reading a novel book?
