20. Buatlah contoh kalimat present continuous tense (Kalimat positif,Kalimat negatif,Kalimat tanya)​


Kalimat (+): Ani is playing guitar

kalimat (-): Ani isn't playing guitar

kalimat (?): Is Ani playing guitar?

Present Continuous Tense

Positif  (+)

S + am/is/are + Verb (-ing)

ex : She is sleeping right now

We are studying today

Negatif  (-)

S + am/is/are + not + verb (-ing)

ex : She is not sleeping right now

We are not studying today

Interrogatif  (?)

am/is/are + S + Verb (-ing) + ?

ex : Is she sleeping right now?

Are we studying today?

2. Contoh kalimat positif, negatif, interrogative, dan wh-questions dalam simple present tense.

(+) She eats an apple. (Dia makan sebuah apel)(-) She doesn't eat an apple. (Dia tidak makan sebuah apel)(?) Does she eat an apple? (Apakah dia makan sebuah apel?)(wh-q) What does she eat? (Apa yang dia makan?)

Simak penjelasan berikut.

Pembahasan

Simple Present Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan, kebenaran umum, rutinitas, kenyataan, atau pendapat.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: usually, often, always, sometimes, everyday, every week.

Bentuk simple present:

(+) Subject + verb1 / verb1+s/es(-) Subject + does + NOT + verb1(?) Do/Does + subject + verb1 .... ?

Present Continuous Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang sedang dilakukan saat ini atau sedang berlangsung.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: now, at present, right now, at the moment, this time.

Bentuk present continuous:

(+) Subject + is/am/are + verb1+ing(-) Subject + is/am/are + NOT + verb1+ing(?) Is/Am/Are + subject + verb1+ing .... ?

Wh-Questions adalah pertanyaan yang diawali dengan what, why, when, where, who, dan how. Kata pertanyaan tersebut diikuti pola pertanyaan interrogative yang diakhiri hingga kata kerja.

Present perfect tense dipakai untuk menyatakan perbuatan atau situasi yang dimulai di masa lampau dan sudah selesai atau masih terjadi atau masih ada hubungannya dengan masa sekarang.

Formula untuk positive:

Subject + has/have + Verb 3

Formula untuk negative:

Subject + has/have (not) + Verb 3

Formula untuk interrogative:

Has/have + subject + Verb 3 (?)

Has: She, he, it

Have: I, you, they, we

Contoh kalimat:

(+) I have eaten breakfast.

(-) I have not eaten breakfast.

(?) Have I eaten breakfast?

(+) She has done her homework.

(-) She has not done her homework.

(?) Has she done her homework?

(-) Pizza was not going to be eaten by Ratna.
