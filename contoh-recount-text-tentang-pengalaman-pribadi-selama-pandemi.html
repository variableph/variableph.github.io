After seeing the sun raised, we had to go back home. Getting down the mountain was not as hard as the climbing process because we only spent a few energy and time even we had to be more careful to do this process.

However, it was great experience i had ever done so far.

Reorientation

After all, that was my great experience I had ever have.

Finally, my father tought me to ride a motorcycle in a field near the house. Father gave instructions on how to drive it. First, my father led me from behind and I was riding on my father. Over time, I want to try it myself. I was happy and proud to be riding a motorcycle.

A few days later, I tried to ride a motorcycle on the narrow street of my house. I was nervous when there was another motorcycle in front of me. Unexpectedly, my motorcycle hit the wall which made me falled from the motorbike.

16. buatlah recount text tentang pengalaman pribadi ​


"Cerita Singkat Pendudukan Jepang di Indonesia"

...

Pendudukan Jepang di Indonesia terjadi pada tahun 1942 sampai 1945, pada masa Perang Dunia II. Invasi Jepang ke Indonesia dimulai pada bulan Desember 1941 tetapi Jepang berhasil mengalahkan Belanda sepenuhnya pada bulan Maret 1942.

Selama pendudukan Jepang, banyak orang Indonesia yang disiksa dan mengalami perbudakan, penangkapan sewenang-wenang, eksekusi, dan banyak kejahatan perang lainnya. Banyak orang yang dipaksa menjadi buruh yang disebut romusha. Kebanyakan romusha menderita kelaparan dan penyakit yang menyebabkan mereka mati.

Jepang mengorganisir sebuah panitia kemerdekaan Indonesia pada bulan Maret 1945 dan berjanji akan memberikan kemerdekaan kepada Indonesia pada bulan Agustus di tahun yang sama. Namun demikian, sebelum Jepang dapat memenuhi janji tersebut, Jepang menyerah kepada AS setelah pengeboman Hiroshima dan Nagasaki. Untuk mengambil momentum tidak ada yang memerintah negara, Indonesia memproklamasikan kemerdekaan pada 17 Agustus 1945.

Itulah berakhirnya pendudukan Jepang, yang dilaporkan telah menewaskan empat juta orang Indonesia selama periode tersebut.

30. buatlah recount text tentang pengalaman pribadi (3 paragraf)​


Recount text on personal experience

Orientation:

Have you ever seen a ghost? Like in horror stories and movies? Indonesia where we live is full of legends about ghosts. And the experience I had was really scary.

Events:

It was night, Friday night. I came home late at night. At that time I had just finished studying at a friend's house, doing math homework together. About ten o'clock at night I just got home. At that time the road I passed was very quiet and dark. To the left of the road is a wide and deserted field. There was a part of the field that was covered with a lot of bushes. There are no lights on the street. For some reason, every time people install lights in the area it always breaks. I walked without looking left or right. The darkness seemed to want to pounce on me from behind, crush and then vomit me out. I continued to walk cautiously. When suddenly...The voice came from the tall bushes. Sounds like footsteps of people stepping on dry leaves and bushes. I don't know why my steps are getting heavier. When the voice got closer and faster I ran as fast as I could. Suddenly a force grabbed my clothes from behind. I was shocked. No matter how hard I tried to break free, the tug just got stronger. I screamed. Harder I pulled my shirt, I don't want to die here. Then suddenly...My shirt was torn, then I heard the sound of a tree branch breaking, I fell forward. Then from the bush ran a little weasel, frightened by my screams.

Conclusion:

I sat up and slowly began to realize. My shirt caught in a tree branch, and the sound in the bushes was that of a weasel. I laughed. Very stupid. I think I should stop watching horror movies.

Orientation yaitu pengenalan awal tentang apa yang akan diceritakan. Events yang menceritakan pengalaman. Conclusion yang merupakan kesimpulan. Pelajari lebih lanjut
