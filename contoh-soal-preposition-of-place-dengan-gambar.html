6. Fungsi dari preposition of place


untuk mengetahui letak tempatpreposition of place and position adalah sebuah preposisi yang digunakan/memiliki fungsi untuk menunjukkan sebuah tempat atau posisi yang dimaksud. Tempat dan posisi yang dimaksud ditampilkan dengan kombinasi antara preposition dan noun(pharse)yang membentuk prepositional phrase.

22. kinds of preposition of place​


Jawaban:

Prepositions of place show the relationship of place between the nouns to the other parts of a sentence. On, at, in, by, from, to, towards, up, down, across, between, among, through, in front of, behind, above, over, under, below, etc. are the most common prepositions of place/direction.

24. Percakapan tentang preposition of place


A: Have you seen my English book?

B: It may be on the desk inside your room.

A: I checked it there, but I didn't find it.

B: It might be under your bed. Have you checked it there?

A: No I haven't.

5 minutes later...

A: Yeah, the book was under the bed. Thank you.

B: You're welcome.
