SUBJECT + VERB ( S/ES ) + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT  ( VERBAL SENTENCE )

RIGHT NOW, NOW, STILL, AT THE MOMENT

1. Present tense
Pattern :
Untuk kalimat verbal (dengan kata kerja) :
S + V +s/es + o/c. (+)
S + do/does + not + V + o/c.
ex :
I go to school every day by bus.
He plays football with his friend every sunday. 
They don't come to course.

Untuk kalimat Nominal (dengan kata benda, kata sifat)
S + to be + noun/adj (+)
S + to be + not + noun/adj (-)
ex :
She is so cute.
I am a student.
They are not a good friend.

Present Continous
S +to be + Ving + o/c. (+)
S + tobe + not + Ving + o/c.
Ex
I am visiting market now.
He is playing football with his friend. 
They are coming to my house.

27. Apa contoh kata simple present continuous tense dan simple present​


Present continuous tense adalah tense yang berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan aksi yang sedang dilakukan pada masa sekarang (ketika berbicara) dan sesuatu di masa depan. Kalimat present continuous dibentuk oleh auxiliary verb “be” seperti am, is, are dan diikuti oleh present participle (bentuk V 1+ing).
