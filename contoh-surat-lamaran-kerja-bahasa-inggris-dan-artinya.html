Dalam membuat surat lamaran kerja, terdapat beberapa hal yang perlu di cantumkan, yaitu :

Tempat, dan tanggal : Bandung, October 12, 2018Penerima surat, yang berisi nama dan alamat lengkap penerima suratSapaan pembuka,  biasanya berupa : Dear, CarolinaNama penulis, pendidikan terakhir dan jurusan penulis. Sebagai pembuka ada baiknya untuk menuliskan umur, posisi pekerjaan yang akan dilamar, dan kemampuan/skill penulis. Apabila penulis mendapatkan informasi lowongan pekerjaan melalui media tertentu seperti koran, majalah, atau media lainnya, ada baiknya untuk menambahkannya pada paragraf pertama.Pengalaman kerja penulis, apabila ada.Apa yang penulis harapkan ketika penuis melamar untuk pekerjaan tersebut : "through this job, I would like to gain more knowledge on rural development in Indonesia."Lampiran yang dicantumkan : "As your consideration, along with this letter I am enclosing my Curriculum vitae, Job application form, Transcript, and IELTS Score Report. "Penutup, yang berisilan harapan atau keingingan penulis setelah penuis mengirim surat tersebut : "I really hope to be given the opportunity to interview at your convenient time.  Thank you for your attention and consideration. "Salam penutup. Salam penutup bisa berupa "Sincerely" atau "Regards" atau "Best regards" yang kemudian diikuti nama penulis.

Penjelasan:

Application Letter

Application letter adalah surat bisnis yang bertujuan untuk melamar pekerjaan kedalam sebuah perusahaan.

Generic Structure dari Application Letter adalah:

1. salutation : Berisi salam pembuka kepada HRD perusahaan atau orang yang bertanggung jawab dalam perekrutan.

2. introduction : Berisi pembukaan. Biasanya berbentuk perkenalan diri atau biodata pelamar.

3. main message or body: Berisi maksud dan tujuan penulis bahwa dia akan melamar sebuah pekerjaan dalam perusahaan tersebut.

4. the close : Berisi kalimat penutupan. Biasanya berbentuk harapan agar si penulis dapat diterima dalam perusahaan tersebut.

5. complimentary close: Berisi salam penutup dan ucapan terimakasih.

Language features dari Application Letter:

1. use the present tense

2. use the verbs to indicate actions and feelings

3. use the future tense for suggesting solutions to problems the job

29. Contoh surat lamaran kerja shopkeeper bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

I am writing to apply for the job opening as a Shop Assistant in your company. Please see the enclosed resume for my references and the details of my experience. I’m sure you will see that I am very qualified to fill the job. I am confident I will be an asset your company.

I have been working as a parts clerk in an auto supply shop for the past three years. My job has me accomplishing a wide variety of tasks every day, from greeting customers and answering phones to driving a forklift and setting up parts displays. I work efficiently and with very little supervison. I have excellent relationships with my customers, who come back to see me time and again when they need help with auto repair. And my track record for safety is spotless.

In high school I completed a vocational program that included some automotive engine repair. I have also been restoring my own vehicles for years – first with my father and brothers when I was just a little guy, and then on cars I bought for myself once I got my license.

I have good work habits. I keep my tools in good shape and my work area tidy. I know how to use a computer to perform basic tasks. I have a clean driving abstract, and I am working towards my commercial driver’s license.

It would be a pleasure to meet with you to discuss my qualifications. You can reach me at 021-234-567 days or evenings.

Respectfully,

Thank you
