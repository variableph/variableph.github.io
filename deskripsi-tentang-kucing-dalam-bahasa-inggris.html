Penjelasan:

A cat is a small carnivorous mammal that is well-known for its adorable appearance and unique personality. Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years and have become one of the most beloved pets in the world.

Cats have a soft and furry coat that varies in color and texture. They have a muscular body and sharp claws that they use for hunting and playing. The ears of cats are also a notable feature, with their pointed shape and excellent hearing abilities.

In terms of personality, cats are often described as independent and curious creatures. They are known for their agility and playfulness, often chasing after toys and jumping up to high places. Despite their independent nature, cats can also be affectionate and enjoy spending time with their owners.

Cats are also known for their grooming habits, spending a significant portion of their day cleaning themselves. They are meticulous creatures, using their rough tongue to lick their fur clean and keeping themselves tidy.

Overall, cats are fascinating creatures that have captured the hearts of people all over the world. Their unique personalities, adorable appearance, and fascinating habits make them a popular choice for pet owners.

10. DESKRIPSI TENTANG KUCING DALAM BAHASA INGGRIS DENGAN TERJEMAHAN BAHASA INDONESIA


Descriptive Text

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umumMenggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatuPembahasan

My Lovely Persian Cat

If you want to pet an animal, I suggest you to pet a Persian cat. Persian cats are the most beautiful cats, I think. And from all Persian cats, Georgy, is the cutest and the sweetest. Here is the description of my Persian cat, Georgy.

Georgy is my beautiful Persian cat. Georgy has a large round head, a short nose, big eyes, full cheeks, and small ears with rounded tips. He has short legs and a short tail. He is so fat with a long, thick, shiny golden coat with a fine texture. He is so cute.

He likes to sit beside me when I watch TV. He likes to enjoy TV commercials, especially cat food commercials.  Playing a game on my tablet is another hobby of his. He likes to play Tetris on my gadget. He always pads it with his paws like he wants to win the game. Unfortunately, he never does. He is so sweet, isn't he?

I love Georgy so much. That is why I always take care of it. The most important thing to understand about caring for a Persian is the need for daily grooming. That long, beautiful coat doesn’t stay clean and tangle-free on its own. It must be gently but thoroughly combed and brushed every day, and regular bathing—at least once a month—is a good idea.

Terjemahan

Kucing Persia Ku Tercinta

Jika kamu ingin memelihara binatang, saya sarankan untuk memelihara kucing Persia. Kucing Persia adalah kucing paling cantik, menurut ku. Dan dari semua kucing Persia, Georgy adalah yang paling imut dan manis. Berikut adalah deskripsi dari kucing Persia ku, Georgy.  

Georgy is adalah kucing Persia emas ku yang cantik. Georgy mempunyai kepala bundar yang besar, hidung kecil, mata besar, pipi tembem, dan telinga yang kecil.  Dia memiliki kaki-kaki yang pendek dan ekor yang pendek juga. Dia sangat gemuk dengan bulu pajang, tebal dan bersinar yang bertekstur lembut. Dia itu sangat imut.

Dia suka duduk disamping ku ketika aku menonton TV. Dia suka menonton komersial TV, khususnya komersial makanan kucing. Bermain game di tablet ku adalah hobinya yang lian. Dia suak bermain Tetris di gadget ku. Dia selalu menekan-nekan tablet dengan kakinya seakan dia akan memenangkan permainan itu. Sayangnya, dia tidak pernah menang. Dia sungguh manis, bukan?

Saya sangat menyukai Georgy. Itulah mengapa saya selalu merawatnya dengan baik. Hal yang paling pentung untuk dipaham tentang merawat kucing Persia adalah kebutuhannya untuk dirawat setiap hari. Bulu panjang cantiknya itu tidak bertahan bersih dan bebas dari kusut. Bulu tersebut harus disisir dan disikat dengan lembut setiap hari, dan dimandikan teratur paling tidak sebulan sekali.

Cats are also called domestic cats [1] [2] or house cats (scientific name: Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus) are a type of carnivorous mammal from the family Felidae. The word "cat" usually refers to "cat" that has been tamed, [6] but can also refer to "big cats" like lions and tigers.

Cats have mingled with human life for at least 6,000 years BC, from the skeletons of cats on the island of Cyprus. [7] Ancient Egyptians from 3,500 BC have used cats to keep mice or other rodents away from barns that store crops. [8]

At present, cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. [9] Cats whose lineages are officially recorded as breed cats or pure breeds, such as persia, siam, manx, and sphinx. Cats like these are usually bred in official animal rearing centers.
