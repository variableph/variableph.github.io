3. The singer of the song above is Joe Walsh. Tell me the biography of Joe Walsh!

(Penyanyi dari lagu di atas adalah Joe Walsh. Ceritakan biografi tentang Joe Walsh!)

Answer: Joe Walsh (Joseph Fidler Walsh) was born on November 20,1947 in Wichita, Kansas. He is a singer, song writer, and an American rock guitarist. He has been a member of three successful rock bands: James Gang, Eagles, and Ringo Starr.

"Hotel California" is his first album with the band Eagles in 1975. In 1998, the guitar solos on the track "Hotel California" by Walsh and Don Felder was selected as the best guitar solos of all time.

In 1992, Walsh released what appeared to be his final album (until 2012), Songs for a Dying Planet, his tenth solo studio album.
