1. Have you ever written an email to a penpal?when?


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: personal letter
Kata kunci: penpal, informal letter
Pembahasan:

Yes, I have written an email and even a conventional letter to a penpal. It was about 10 years ago. I've got couple of penpals, but the ones who had been close to me were all of them from Eastern Europe. They were Elena from Ukraine, Tatiana from Russia and Nina from Hungary. The longest one I knew was Elena, who has moved to Australia and married to her Australian boyfriend now, we used to exchange pictures and gifts. She once gave me a Russian novel in English, Leo Tolstoy's Anna Karenina, which I hadn't been able to find it here. Meanwhile, the nicest one was Tatiana, who is now already married to her Polish boyfriend and living in Krakow, Poland. We were very close for only a year and a half before she was engaged and used to exchange postcards and conventional letters to each other. The most current one was Nina, whom I knew about just 3 years ago. Unfortunately, we have been busy in our life that we haven't been able to contact more often.

Arti dari pertanyaan tersebut:

Jawaban dari pertanyaan tersebut:

"Yes, I have, (keterangan waktu di sini)"

atau "No, I haven't"

Penjelasan:

Karena pertanyaan tersebut digunakan untuk menanyakan apakah kamu sudah pernah atau belum maka jawaban yang tepat harus menggunakan "..., I have/haven't" sesuai dengan pertanyaannya. Lalu untuk jawabannya, kamu bisa mengisi keterangan waktunya dengan "yesterday" atau kalau ingin menambahkan tanggal yang spesifik, harus menambahkan "on", contohnya "on 29th of July, 2021", atau bisa juga kalau kamu hanya ingin menambahkan waktu/jam yang spesifik, kalau begitu kamj harus menambahkan "at", contohnya "at 12.55 PM".
