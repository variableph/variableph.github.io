Rumus Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

8. Passive Voice [Future Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will be + being + Verb3

9. Passive Voice [Future Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will have + been + Verb3

29. task 2 change the following active sentences into passive1. he manages the local branch of a national shoe retailer2. the company has advertised the job opportunity in the national newspaper.3. he developed time management tools for staff.4. she will enclose her resume in the application letter.5. linda is writing an application letter for the position as a secretary.


║ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE║

Answer

1. The local branch of a national shoe retailer is managed by him.

2. The job opportunity has been advertised in the national newspaper by the company.

3. Time management tools were developed for staff by him

4. Her resume will be enclosed in the application letter by her

5. An application letter is being written for the position as a secretary by Linda.

Discussion

Passive voice is focused on the object and the action rather than the doer of the action. When we want to change the active voice to the passive voice, keep in mind that we must put the object at the beginning of sentence as well as the action. Meanwhile, the doer of the action will be the agent in the passive voice.Harap diperhatikan baik-baik, bahwa tidak semua kata kerja yang terlampir dalam kalimat aktif bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive. Pastikan bahwa kalimat aktif tersebut termasuk transitive verb ( dimana kata kerjanya memerlukan obyek untuk memperjelas informasi ). Passive voice tidak bisa merubah kata kerja tanpa obyek ( Intransitive verb ).  Contoh : Go, sleep, occur, happen .Ciri khas penerapan kalimat passive BE + VERB 3 ( past participle )Dalam perubahan dari kalimat aktif ke passive, obyek kalimat aktif akan diposisikan sebagai Subyek dalam kalimat pasif. Sedangkan Subyek kalimat aktif akan diposisikan sebagai obyek preposisi ( Agent ). Passive voice mengikuti bentuk tenses dan tidak merubah arti sebenarnya dari kalimat aktif.Harap diperhatikan pronoun nya ( kata ganti orang ). Pronoun akan berubah berdasarkan posisi kelas kata ( parts of speech ) dalam kalimat pasif.

Berdasarkan teks di atas, yang perlu diperhatikan dalam merubah kalimat aktif  ke pasif adalah dengan memperhatikan obyek dalam kalimat aktif dimana akan diletakkan sbg posisi subyek dlm kalimat pasif, lalu bentuk tenses dari kata kerja masing-masing kalimat, perubahan pronoun berdasarkan posisi kelas kata, dan subyek kalimat aktif dimana akan dirubah sbg Agent.

1. Noun phrase/ obyek  "the local branch of a national shoe retailer" diletakkan di posisi sbg subyek kalimat pasif. Kata "Manages" merupakan bentukan dari kata kerja dengan pola simple present tense. Sedangkan, "he" diletakkan di posisi obyek preposisi dan dirubah menjadi "him" (objective pronoun)

Rumus kalimat pasif simple present tense :

S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb 3 + by + Agent....

2. Noun phrase/ obyek  "the job opportunity" diletakkan di posisi sbg subyek kalimat pasif. Kata "has advertised" merupakan bentukan dari kata kerja dengan pola present perfect tense. Sedangkan, "the company" diletakkan di posisi obyek preposisi.

Rumus kalimat pasif present perfect tense :

S+has/have+been+verb 3 + by + Agent....

3. Noun phrase/ obyek  "the time management tools" diletakkan di posisi sbg subyek kalimat pasif. Kata "developed" merupakan bentukan dari kata kerja dengan pola simple past tense. Sedangkan, "he" diletakkan di posisi obyek preposisi dan dirubah menjadi "him" (objective pronoun)

Rumus kalimat pasif simple past :

S+tobe(was/were)+verb 3+ by + Agent....

4. Noun phrase/ obyek  "her resume" diletakkan di posisi sbg subyek kalimat pasif. Kata "will enclose" merupakan bentukan dari kata kerja dengan pola simple future tense. Sedangkan, "she" diletakkan di posisi obyek preposisi ( Agent ) dan dirubah menjadi "her" (objective pronoun).

Rumus kalimat pasif simple future :

S+will+be+verb 3 + by + Agent.....

5. Noun phrase/ obyek  "an application letter" diletakkan di posisi sbg subyek kalimat pasif. Kata "is writing" merupakan bentukan dari kata kerja dengan pola present continuous tense. Sedangkan, "Linda" diletakkan di posisi obyek preposisi ( Agent ).

Rumus kalimat pasif present continuous :

S+tobe(is/am/are)+being+verb 3 + by + Agent....
