Understanding Narrative Text

1. When Sangkuriang saw Dayang Sumbi, he proposed to marry her. Was he a bad son to his mother?

Yes, he was a bad son to his mother because son should never marry his own biological mother.

2. Why did he want to marry her?

Sangkuriang wanted to marry Dayang Sumbi because she was a beautiful woman and he fell in love with her.

3. Why did Dayang Sumbi ask Sangkuriang to build a lake and a boat in one night?

Dayang Sumbi asked Sangkuriang to do it because she realized that Sangkuriang is her son and she didn’t want to marry him. So she gave him two impossible mission hoping that Sangkuriang won’t be able to finish them in one night, so they will not get married.

4. Why could not Sangkuriang finish the boat?

Sangkuriang couldn’t finish the boat because Dayang Sumbi was asking the woman to pounding rice and rooster think that is a morning, so they crow.

5. How did the boat get upside down?

Sangkuriang was angry because he failed to finish the mission, so he opened the dam and kicked the boat. The boat is rolled back and turn into Tangkuban Perahu mountain.

Pembahasan

Cerita tentang terjadinya Tangkuban Perahu adalah salah satu jenis teks narasi.

Narrative text adalah jenis bacaan yang berupa cerita khayalan, kisah nyata yang direkayasa, atau dongeng.

Struktur narrative text:

Orientation (pendahuluan) - pada bagian ini biasanya diceritakan awal mula dan perkenalan tokoh-tokohnya beserta tempat dan waktu kejadian.Complication - bagian ini menceritakan tentang ketika permasalahan antar tokoh karakter muncul.Resolution - bagaimana penyelesaian masalah pada bagian complication diatasi di bagian ini.Re-orientation - adalah rangkuman dan penutup dari cerita narasi.

Tujuan: Untuk menghibur para pembaca.

Karakteristik:

Menggunakan pola kalimat lampau (simple past tense)Diawali dengan kata keterangan waktu (adverbial of time) contohnya seperti long time ago, once upon a time, dll.Menggunakan kata sambung (conjunction) seperti and then, before, after, dll.Pelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang narrative text:
