21. work in pairs.complete the dialog with the words in brackets.change the words into correct forms.​


Jawaban:

What (your brother - do)?

What does you brother do?

He (be) a radio broadcaster.

He is a radio broadcaster.

Where (he - work)?

Where does he work?

He (work) for a radio station.

He works for a radio station.

What programs (he-usually broadcast)?

What programs does he usually broadcast?

Many programs, especially related to sports and health.

(he-enjoy) his job?

Does he enjoy his job?

He does though he (still continue) his studies at university.

He does though he still continues his studies at university.

(you-mean) he is studying while working?

Do you mean he is studying while working?

Yes, you (be) right. He (study) ...

Yes, you are right. He studies ...

Penjelasan:

Dialog di atas membahas tentang aktivitas seseorang sehari-hari. Tense yang digunakan adalah simple present tense.

(+) Subject + verb 1

(-) Subject + do/does + not + verb 1

(?) Do/does + subject + verb 1?
