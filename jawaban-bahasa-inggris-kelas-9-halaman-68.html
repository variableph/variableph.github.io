3. Tolong jawabin bahasa inggris kelas 9 semester 2 halaman 68-67 pilgan aja​


Narrative Text

Narrative text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menghibur pembacanya. Terdapat beberapa jenis narrative text, yaitu:

1. Fable

2. Legend

3. Fairy tale

4. Horror

5. Romance

6. Mystery

7. Myth

8. dan lain lain

Generic Structure dari Narrative Text adalah:

• Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.(berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat dan waktu terjadinya cerita (siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana)

• Complication : Where the problems in the story developed. (Permasalahan muncul / mulai terjadi dan berkembang)

• Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved. Masalah selesai, secara baik “happy ending” ataupun buruk “bad ending”.

• Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story

Language Features dari Narrative Text adalah:

• Use Past tense (killed, drunk, etc)

• Use adverb of time (Once upun a time, one day, etc)

• Use time conjunction (when, then, suddenly, etc)

• Use specific character. The character of the story is specific, not general. (Cinderella, Snow White, Alibaba, etc)

• Use action verbs. A verb that shows an action. (killed, dug, walked, etc)

• Use direct speech. It is to make the story lively. (Snow White said,”My name is Snow White). The direct speech uses present tense.

Pembahasan

Soal nomor 6-12 berdasarkan teks Narrative di atas:

No. 7.: What was samosir before married? He was a farmer (B)

No. 9: What did Samosir promise to his wife? He would not tell anyone that his wife was a fish (D)

No. 10: Why was Samosir very angry? Because his daughter ate his lunch (B)

No. 11: Why did Samosir's wife ask her daughter to run up the hills? A huge disaster would come (A)

Fungsi tulang

Tulang hasta fungsinya:Membentuk lengan bawah sebagai alat gerak manusia Serta menghubungkan lengan atas dengan pergelangan tangan.

Tulang atas Fungsinya:Untuk tempat menempel otot utama yang menggunakan bahu dan siku.

Tulang pengumpil Fungsinya:Anggota alat gerak bagian atas yang akan menghubungkan bagian siku dengan ibu jari/jempol.

Tulang pergelangan tangan Fungsinya:Untuk menghubungkan tulang jari.

Tulang telapak tangan fungsinya:Pemberi bentuk tangan,menghubungkan tulang jari dengan pangkal tangan.

Tulang paha Fungsinya:Menghubungkan tulang bagian pinggul dan lutut,untuk menyangga pada saat berjalan.

Tulang kering Fungsinya:Menghasilkan sel-sel darah merah dan menyediakan mineral,partikel kalsium,dan posfor.

Tulang betis Fungsinya:Untuk menyangga badan agar kuat dan seimbang dan,tempat melekatnya otot kaki.

Tulang pergelangan kaki Fungsinya:Menggerakan kaki ketika berjalan,membantu naik turun kaki.

Tulang telapak kaki Fungsinya:Sebagai pijakan agar saat kita berjalan seimbang.

Tulang jari kaki Fungsinya:Membantu tubuh berjalan,berlari/berdiri agar tubuh tetap seimbang tidak jatuh.

24. buku bahasa inggris bab 4 kelas 7 halaman 68 ktsp 2013


Direction menggunakan konjungsi untuk mendeskripsikan location, seperti

1. in front of = di depan

2. behind = di belakang

3. on the right = di sebelah kanan

4. on the left = di sebelah kiri

5. between = di antara

6. next to = di sebelah

7. across from = di seberang

8. on the corner = di pojok

Pembahasan

1. the bank is on the left, next to the post office, across from the tax office.

(bank di sebelah kiri kantor pos, di sebelah kantor pos, di seberang kantor pajak.)

2. the post office is next to the bank, across from the tax office.

(kantor pos di sebelah bank, di seberang kantor pajak)

3. The tax office is on the corner, next to the bank, across from the post office.

(Kantor pajak di pojok, di sebelah bank, di seberang kantor pos)

4. The school is on the right, next to the post office, across from the police station.

(sekolah di sebelah kanan, di sebelah kantor pos, di seberang kantor polisi).

5. The hospital is on the left, in front of police station, across from the post office.

(Rumah sakit di sebelah kiri, di depan kantor polisi, di seberang kantor pos)

6. The police station is on the left, across from hospital)

(kantor polisi di sebelah kiri, di seberang rumah sakit)
