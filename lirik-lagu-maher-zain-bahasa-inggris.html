10. Lagu Maher Zein, insya Allah Bahasa inggris, cari liriknya yang ada kalimat adjective, Subject, dan apa pesan dari lagu itu? Catatan : Jawab Pakai Bahasa Inggris


Answer: 

In this popular pop religious song by Swedish artist, Maher Zein , some of the adjective sentences (the sentence which has adjectives in it) are
- You're so confused
- It's way too late
- He's never far away
- you're so alone

Meanwhile, the subject of this song is us the listener and all Moslem in the world who are familiar with it. This song is more like an encouragement for us to have faith in Allah, no matter what. 
The message in this song is that we should keep praying and believing in Allah's blessing despite the misfortune, or feeling despair as Allah the Almighty God would listen your prayers and grant your wish whenever it's considered proper in time.

19. contoh descriptve text bahasa inggris tentang maher zain


Maher Zain, born 16 July 1981 is a Muslim Swedish R&B singer, songwriter and music producer of Lebanese origin. With more Facebook fans than any other Muslim artist, award-winning singer-songwriter Maher Zain is one of the biggest stars in modern Islamic music. He released his debut album Thank You Allah, an internationally successful album produced by Awakening Records, in 2009. He released his follow-up album Forgive Me in April 2012 under the same production company.

20. unsur kebahasaan dari lagu insya allah maher zain


Kelas:SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: literary analysis
Pembahasan: 

From the language, it can be seen that the tense mostly used in this song is present simple apart from several sentences in future tense like 'you'll find a way' or 'we'll find a way.'
Moreover, this song contains some imperative such as ' don't despair', 'never lose hope', 'put your trust in him' beside sentences stating the feeling after being sinful or making mistakes in life before praying to Allah, the Almighty God.
