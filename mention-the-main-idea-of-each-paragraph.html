1. mention the main idea of each paragraph 1,2,3,4​


In reading comprehension, finding out the main idea of each paragraph is as important as knowing the main idea of the whole text. In fact, it is inseparable as both are connected.

Penjelasan:

To find the main ideas in each paragraph, one needs to read carefully. Here are some patterns you can notice about main idea

It is normally put at the beginning of the passage, can be the first or first two sentence.it can also be seen at the end of the paragrah, which can be considered as a conclusion as well.it can be all over the place in the passage (usually in narrative text)

Here are the main idea of each paragraph.

the main idea is at the last sentence of par.1 (The word 'plasma' refers to ....illuminated screen)the main idea is at the first sentence of par.2 (Plasma displays wide ....resolution pictures)the main idea is at the first sentence of par.1 (The plasma TV consumes....scenes)the main idea is at the last sentence of the text (it is the reason why plasma TV is efficient)
