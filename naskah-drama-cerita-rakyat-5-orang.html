(STORY PART 1 ) :

Once a long time ago, there were a couple of farmer. They lived happily but there is only one of their problem, they didn't have a child! Until one day they decided to pray to the mountain god.

(Wife) : Oh the mighty god that guarding this mountain, can you hear our prayers?
(Husband) : If you can hear our prayers, please grant us a child!

They kept praying and praying, until suddenly they heard a loud voice.

(Giant) : Muahahaha! Lowly humans, I can grant your wish! But you must promise me one thing!
(Husband) : What's the promise we must keep?
(Giant) : Easy! just give me back that child when she is already 17 years old!
(Husband) : How about that, my wife? Must we accept it or not?
(Wife) : Just accept it, I do really want a child from long time ago!

Then they agreed with the giant's requirement and then the giant gave them something.

(Giant) : Here! Plant this cucumber seed and you will see a miracle happen!
(Husband) : Don't joking around! I'm serious here! Give us the child!
(Wife) : Ssshh, just do what he said before, we even didn't try it yet, right?
(Giant) : Muahahaha, what a funny man! Try to hear what your wife says, then see the miracle! Now i will go, let's meet seventeen years later, MUAHAHAHA!

A golden cucumber that it beared, seems weird, the couple of farmer then decided to cut the cucumber into half. What a surprise, there was a female baby in the cucumber.

(Wife) : Look, my husband! It's a baby! The giant didn't lie to us!
(Husband) : Yes, my wife! A miracle really does happen. Now what shall we name this child?
(Wife) : Timun Mas! Because she is from a golden cucumber!

Finally they named her Timun Mas. Timun Mas grew into a beautiful girl and so kind to the villagers in her village, also became a lovely daughter for her parent. She lived happily in that village, until one day before she became seventeen, her parents called her.

(Husband) : Sorry, Timun. But you must go now! No time to explain! Go as far as you can! Now!
(Timun Mas) : Why Dad? Mom? Why? At least give me a reason why must i go!
(Wife) : I'm sorry my daughter, I will reveal the truth now. You're actually not my daughter. I made a deal with a giant 17 years ago. You know what happen after that right?
(Timun Mas) : So what must I do now? Mom? Dad?
(Husband) : Go to a lake at the center of this village, you will meet a fairy there. Now, go!
