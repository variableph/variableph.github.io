7. Dayang Sumbi was a and kind


Jawaban:Dayang Sumbi was a beautiful and kind hearted princess, but sometimes she was very lazy. Her hobby was weaving cloth. One day her weaving tool fell. Tumang, a male dog, came to bring her ... back to her. As she had promised, she married him. Tumang was actually a man who had been cursed by a ... to become a dog. But sometimes he could turn back to a normal man. Dayang Sumbi and Tumang got one son. His name was Sangkuriang. He did not know that Tumang was his father because he was a dog whenever he was with him. Tumang always accompanied Sangkuriang whenever he went hunting in the woods.

Pembahasan

Pada pilihan jawaban, ada dua kata yang tidak ada, yaitu tool dan witch.

Pada kalimat "... her weaving tool fell. Tumang, a ... dog, came to bring her ... back to her", seharusnya yang benar adalah

One day her weaving tool fell. Tumang, a male dog, came to bring her tool back to her.

Pada kalimat "Tumang was actually a ... who had been cursed by a ... to become a dog", seharusnya yang benar adalah

Tumang was actually a man who had been cursed by a witch to become a dog.

17. 1)Who was Dayang Sumbi? Describe her.​


Jawaban:

Dayang Sumbi is is the main female character in the folktale The Legend of Sangkuriang. She was the daughter of King Sungging Perbangkara and the mother of the main male character Sangkuriang. She is depicted in the story as a woman with eternal beauty and youth that even Sangkuriang himself does not recognize her when he met her again after his years of journey around the world and wanted to marry her. This forbidden love story between mother and son eventually led to creation of Mount Tangkuban Perahu.

source: indonesianfolktales.fandom
