1. put the verbs in brackets with "should" or "should have".


Jawaban:

1. The pupils are bored with English lesson. The teacher SHOULD make the lesson more interesting for pupils.

(Murid murid bosan dengan pelajaran Bahasa Inggris. guru tersebut harus membuat pelajarannya lebih menyenangkan untuk murid - murid tersebut.)

2. Look at the vase. It's broken into pieces now. You SHOULD HAVE BEEN CAREFUL. It was an expensive Chinese vase.

(Lihat vas bunga itu. Vasnya sangat hancur. Kamu SEHARUSNYA berhati - hati. Itu adalah vas bunga mahal dari China)

3. Watch out! A car is coming! You SHOULD BE CAREFUL when cross the street.

(Awas! Mobil mau lewat! Kamu harus berhati - hati kalau menyeberang di jalan)

4. You Shouldn't give the baby a pen to play with it. It's too risky.

(Kamu seharusnya tidak kasih bayi itu pulpen untuk dimainkan. Itu terlalu berbahaya)

5. The man is coughing a lot. He SHOULD NOT smoke so much.

(Pria itu batuk terus. Dia seharusnya tidak merokok terlalu banyak)

6. You SHOULD consult a dictionary. If you don't understand the meaning. (Kamu harus memeriksa di kamus, jika kamu tidak paham dengan artinya)

7. If you had told me about your problem, I would have been glad to help you. You SHOULDN'T HAVE GAVE up easily.

(Jika kamu memberi tahu aku tentang permasalahanmu. Aku akan sangat senang membantu kamu. Kamu TIDAK SEHARUSNYA MENYERAH begitu saja)

8. My car drove into the back of someone else's car. I SHOULDN'T drive the car too close.

(Mobilku menabrak belakang mobil orang lain. Aku harusnya tidak menyetir terlalu dekat)

9. They wrote the word "analyze" with "s", which was UK spelling. They SHOULD HAVE WROTE "z", in US spelling.

(mereka menulis kata "analyze" dengan huruf "s" yang merupakan ejaan Inggris. Mereka SEHARUSNYA menulis dengan huruf "z" yang adalah ejaan Amerika.

10. I didn't order fried rice for my lunch. They SHOULD give me fried noodles.

(Aku tidak memesan nasi goreng. Mereka HARUSnya memberikanku mie goreng)

7. Put the verbs in the brackets into the right forms. • The students usually (have) ceremony every Monday.


Subject : English
Category : Tenses (present simple)

Answer :
the students usually have ceremony every Monday.

Explaination :
adverb of frequency 'usually' is used for the present simple tense.
structure of present simple tense is
S + INFINITIVE
if the subject is the 3rd person singular, the suffix -s is added to the infinitive.

'the students' is plural so the bare infinitive is used.
