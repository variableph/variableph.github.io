23. learn the text and answer the questions below it carefully​


Soal

Has the applicant had a work experience in the same field before applying the job vacancy?What is the purpose of the text?Where did she go to school?What position does she apply for?What should the food and beverage staff behave? Give your opinion!

Jawaban untuk kelima soal di atas adalah:

The applicant has no work experience in the same field before, but she had undergone some practice to give good service to handle the quality of some foods and beverage for international hotel.The purpose of the text is to introduces you to potential employers and highlights your suitability for the position you are applying for.She graduated from Mentari Hotel Education School.She applies for food and beverage staff.Food and beverage staff should be friendly, active, and responsible.

Pembahasan:

Teks di atas termasuk ke dalam materi applicant letter di dalam bahasa Inggris. Applicant letter merupakan sebuah surat yang ditulis kepada sebuah perusahaan ketika seseorang hendak melamar pekerjaan. Tujuan utama dari penulisan surat lamaran kerja atau applicant letter adalah agar pelamar mendapatkan kesempatan untuk diwawancara.
