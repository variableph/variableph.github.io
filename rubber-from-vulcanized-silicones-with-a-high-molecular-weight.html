16. Why oxygen molecules move into the cell? A. The oxygen molecules move from a high to a low concentration by diffusion. B. The oxygen molecules move from a high to a low concentration by osmosis. C. The oxygen molecules move from a low to a high concentration by diffusion. D. The oxygen molecules move from a low to a high concentration by osmosis


Jawaban:

A. The oxygen molecules move from a high to a low concentration by diffusion.

Penjelasan:

Difusi adalah perpindahan partikel terlarut dari tempat dengan konsentrasi tinggi ke tempat dengan konsentrasi rendah. Sementara osmosis adalah perpindahan air dari tempat dengan kepekatan rendah (konsentrasi air tinggi) ke tempat dengan kepekatat tinggi (konsentrasi air rendah).

Oksigen merupakan partikel terlarut. Jadi dapat diketahui apabila oksigen berpindah secara difusi dari konsentrasi tinggi ke rendah.

18. are mass and weight are the same from friction?


Solution

Friction is dependent upon the weight acting between two surfaces. When the mass is more, weight is more and so friction will be more.

Mass and weight may seem like the same thing, but in fact these two measurements are very different. Mass is the total amount of matter, or "stuff," in an object. Weight is the force of gravity on an object
