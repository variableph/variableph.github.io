Soal:

43. I- last holiday - Bogor - visited - my family-in-the- and - Botanical Garden

Make the best arrangement for the words above!

Jawaban:

1. My family and I visited the Botanical Garden in Bogor last holiday

Atau:

2. Last Holiday, My Family and I visited The Botanical Garden in Bogor

Penjelasan:

Karena ada LAST HOLIDAY yg menunjukkan Kegiatan atau Peristiwa lampau maka gunakan Rumus Past Tense

(+) S + VERB 2 + O/C

= My Family and I + VISITED + The Botanical Garden in Bogor

* Last Holiday, bisa di awal ataupun di paling akhir

=== Semoga Membantu ===

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Past Tense

Kode Mapel: 5
