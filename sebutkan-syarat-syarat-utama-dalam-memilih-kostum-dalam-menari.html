————————————————————
Seluruhnya = 9P9
9P9 = 9! / ( 9 - 9 )!
9P9 = 9! / 0!
9P9 = 9!

Permutasi Ari dan Ira Tidak Berdekatan
= 8P8 × 2P2
= 8! / ( 8 - 8 )! × 2! / ( 2 - 2 )!
= 8! / 0! × 2! / 0!
= 8! × 2!

Banyak Cara Permutasi nya .
= 9P9 - 8P8 × 2P2
= 9! - 8! × 2!
= 9 × 8! × 8! - 2
= ( 9 - 2 ) 8!
= 7 × 8! ..... ✔️

Banyak Cara Hitungan nya .
= 7 × 8!
= 7 × 8 . 7 . 6 . 5 . 4 . 3 . 2 . 1
