16. Buatlah contoh undangan graduation dalam Bahasa Inggris dan artinya!


Jawaban:

Malang, April 23, 2018

Hi my best friend Harianto!

I am very pleased to be able to give this invitation letter to you, through this invitation letter, I wish you my best friend Harianto to attend my 17th birthday party. The plan for this birthday party will be held on:

Day / Date: Monday / April 30 2018

Hours: 18.00

Place: My house, Jl. Defense No. 123, Malang

Friend, this birthday party will not be memorable without your presence. I really hope my best friend attends my 6th birthday party. If my best friend is absent, watch out if you meet tomorrow.

Warm greetings from your good friends.

Penjelasan:

Malang, 23 April 2018

Hai sahabatku Harianto!

Senang sekali rasanya bisa memberikan surat undangan ini kepadamu, melalui surat undangan ini saya mengharap kedatanganmu sahabatku Harianto mendatangi pesta kecil ulang tahunku yang ke 17. Rencananya pesta ulang tahun ini akan diselenggarakan pada :

Hari/Tanggal : Senin/30 April 2018

Jam : 18.00

Tempat : Rumahku, Jl. Pertahanan No. 123, Malang

Sahabat, pesta ulang tahun ini tidak akan berkesan tanpa kehadiranmu. Saya berharap sekali sahabatku menghadiri pesta ulang tahunku yang ke 6 ini. Kalau sahabatku tidak hadir, awas ya kalau besok ketemu.

Salam hangat dari Sahabat baikmu,

26. Percakapan graduation 6 orang


Novi, Rahma, Julia are walking past the school garden and they see their classmate Finza, Aldy, Revy sitting in a lawn chair. Novi and friends come over them.Novi: Hi, guys! What are you doing?Revy: We're talking about graduation event for our generation.Julia: The graduation event is still a few months away, why talk about it now?Finza: Because it's still a few months away, we're talking about it now. Because if we near to the graduation ever, and with our graduation exam, there will be many obstacles.Rahma: That's right, you say, Finza. Let's talk about that. So, what theme will we use for the graduation event later?Aldy: The theme we will plan is Day and Prom Night Graduation. So our graduation event will be made in the morning and evening.Novi: Wow, wait a minute! Prom Night? are you kidding? Is not a very impossible thing considering the Headmaster's approval for the event is sometimes still constrained. How about Prom Night? You must be joking.Finza: Therefore, we are discussing now to get approval from the headmaster regarding the Prom Night. Because in terms of sound system, we will lose if rent only in the morning, while the sound system rental provides a full day for the event later.Revy: And also we want the funds we have collected that can be in accordance with the required, no harm. and can be an advantage for us, the students.Julia: All right. Then what about the fund? Do we just expect donations from students and schools? Did the donation from school is not enough to buy consumption last year?Aldy: Well, yes, we discussed it as well. We want our generation, grade 12, to make small food products and market them. then the profit earned can add our graduation fund, what do you think?Rahma: All fine. But remember, our time to make products and market them is there or not. We have to focus on studying for our graduation exam. Don't let due to graduation event, exam get stunted.Revy: That's right. Maybe we're enought of this discussion. You can join our WA group later, to discuss the graduation event. Okay, Novi, Rahma and Julia?Novi, Rahma, Julia: Okay, Revy.Novi: Let's go to class. spirit ya you to persuade our headmaster later hehe.
