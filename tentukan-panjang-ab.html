20. tentukan panjang ab


AE = 7 cm

DE = 3 cm

CD = 8 cm

EF = 9,8 cm

AD = AE + DE

AD = 7 + 3

AD = 10 cm

AB = CD + [(EF - CD)/(DE/AD)]

AB = 8 + [(9,8 - 8)/(3/10)]

AB = 8 + [1,8 × 10/3]

AB = 8 + 6

AB = 14 cm

24. Tentukan panjang AB​


Jawaban:

[tex] {ab}^{2} = {ac}^{2} + {cb}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] {ab}^{2} = {8}^{2} + {15}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] {ab}^{2} = 64 + 225[/tex]

[tex] {ab}^{2} = 289 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]ab = \sqrt{289} [/tex]

[tex]ab = 17 \: cm[/tex]

17 Cm

Pembahasan

~ Panjang Hipotenusa

= A² + B² = C²

= 8² + 15² = C²

= (8 × 8) + (15 × 15) = C²

= 64 + 225 = C²

= 64 + 225 = 289

= √289

= √17

= 17 Cm
