Berdasarkan Rumus Present Tense

Active Voice

➡️ Subjeknya melakukan tindakan dalam kalimatnya

(+) S + V1 + O

1. the customer service officers answer my question

Artinya: petugas pelayanan pelanggan menjawab Pertanyaanku

Passive Voice

➡️ Objeknya diberi tindakan oleh subjek dalam kalimatnya

(+) O + Be (is/am/are) + v3 + By S

=> My question is answered by the customer officers

Artinya: Pertanyaanku dijawab oleh petugas pelayanan pelanggan

18. TaskChange the sentences into passive form!1. He plays the game2. The customer service officers answer my questions.3. My father reads a daily newspaper4. He rides a bike.​


[Passive Voice]

The game is played by him.My questions are answered by the customer service officers.A daily newspaper is read by my father.A bike is ridden by him.

PEMBAHASAN

Passive voice merupakan jenis kalimat yang subjeknya adalah penerima aksi, sedangkan objeknya adalah pelaku dari aksi tersebut.

Rumus utama: Auxiliary + Past participle

↬based on question

Present simple

Subject + to be (is/am/are) + verb 3 + by (agent)

↪number 1

active

He plays the game.

(Dia memainkan suatu permainan)

passive

The game is played by him.

(Suatu permainan dimainkan olehnya)

↪number 2

active

The customer service officers answer my questions.

(Petugas layanan pelanggan menjawab pertanyaanku)

passive

My questions are answered by the customer service officers.

(Pertanyaanku dijawab oleh petugas layanan pelanggan)

↪number 3

active

My father reads a daily newspaper.

(Ayahku membaca sebuah koran harian)

passive

A daily newspaper is read by my father.

(Sebuah koran harian dibaca oleh ayahku)

↪number 4

active

He rides a bike.

(Dia menaiki sebuah sepeda)

passive

A bike is ridden by him.

(Sebuah sepeda dinaiki olehnya)

↬verbs on question

Regular verbs adalah kata kerja beraturan. Bentuk past tense dan past participle-nya ditandai dengan adanya akhiran -d atau -ed.

(1) play - played - played

bermain

(2) answer - answered - answered

menjawab

Irregular verbs adalah kata kerja yang tidak beraturan. Bentuk past tense dan past participle-nya tidak berakhiran -d atau -ed.

(3) read- read - read

membaca

(4) ride - rode - ridden

menaiki, mengendarai
