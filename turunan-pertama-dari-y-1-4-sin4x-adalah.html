16. integral (3 sin4x + 4 cos5x)dx


• IntegraL

-

∫ (3 sin 4x + 4 cos 5x) dx = ___ ?

•••

Sifat IntegraL :

∫ sin ax dx  = -¹/ₐ cos ax + C

∫ cos ax dx = ¹/ₐ sin ax + C

∫ (3 sin 4x + 4 cos 5x) dx

→ 3 . ( -¹/₄ ) cos 4x + 4 . ¹/₅ sin 5x + C

→ -³/₄ cos 4x + ⁴/₅ sin 4x + C

→ ⁴/₅ sin 4x - ³/₄ cos 4x + C

•••

-AL

[tex] \int (3 \sin(4x) + 4 \cos(5x)) \: dx = - \frac{3}{4} \cos(4x) + \frac{4}{5} \sin(5x) + c \\ [/tex]

Rumus Integral Tak Tentu

[tex] \int \sin(ax) \: dx = - \frac{1}{a} \cos(ax) + c \\ \\ \int \cos(ax) \: dx = \frac{1}{a} \sin(ax) + c \\ [/tex]

Pembahasan

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: \int (3 \sin(4x) + 4 \cos(5x)) \: dx \\ \\ = 3 \int \sin(4x) \: dx + 4 \int \cos(5x) \: dx \\ \\ = 3 \: ( - \frac{1}{4} \cos(4x) ) \: + 4 \: ( \frac{1}{5} \sin(5x) ) \: + c \\ \\ = - \frac{3}{4} \cos(4x) + \frac{4}{5} \sin(5x) + c \\ [/tex]

Ingat bahwa sin α + sin β = 2 * sin [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex](α+β) * cos [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex](α-β)
