11. [tex] \mathtt{quiz}[/tex]Hasil dari[tex] \ \sqrt[]{2 + \sqrt{4 + \sqrt{6 + \sqrt{8 + \sqrt{10 + ...} } } } } [/tex]adalah...demon slayer sampahjan nonton:v​


Jawaban

[tex] \sqrt{2 + \sqrt{4 + \sqrt{6 + \sqrt{8 + ...} } } } = 2[/tex]

Pembahasan

Misalkan :

[tex]A = \sqrt{2 + \sqrt{4 + \sqrt{6 + \sqrt{8 + ...} } } } [/tex]

[tex]A = \sqrt{2 + \sqrt{4 + 0+ \sqrt{4 + 2 + 0 + \sqrt{4 + (2 \times 2) + 0 + ...} } } } [/tex]

[tex]B =\sqrt{4 + 0+ \sqrt{4 + 2 + 0 + \sqrt{4 + (2 \times 2) + 0 + ...} } }[/tex]

misalkan a + b > 0 maka :

[tex]a + b = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + 2ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + b(2a + b)} [/tex]

[tex]2a + b = \sqrt{4 {a}^{2} + 4ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + (a + b)(3a + b)} [/tex]

[tex]3a + b = \sqrt{ 9{a}^{2} + 6ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + (2a + b)(4a + b)} [/tex]

sehingga :

[tex]a + b = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + 2ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + b }2a + b = \sqrt{4 {a}^{2} + 4ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + (a + b)3a + b = \sqrt{ 9{a}^{2} + 6ab + {b}^{2} } = \sqrt{ {a}^{2} + (2a + b).....}}[/tex]

nah abistu baru bandingin sama :

[tex]B =\sqrt{4 + 0+ \sqrt{4 + 2 + 0 + \sqrt{4 + (2 \times 2) + 0 + ...} } }[/tex]

maka diperoleh :

a = 2

b = 0

a + b = 2 + 0 = B = 2

A = √(2 + B)

A = √(2 + 2)

A = 2

24. ceritakan film jack the giant slayer menggunakan simpel past


In the Kingdom of Cloister, Jack, a young farm boy, was fascinated by the legend of Erik, an ancient king who defeated an army of invading giants from a realm in the sky by controlling them with a magical crown. At the same time, Princess Isabelle became fascinated with the same legend.

Ten years later, Jack went into town to sell his horse to support his uncle's farm. There, Jack spoted Isabelle and became enamored with her after defending her honor from a group of hooligans. Meanwhile, Lord Roderick returned to his study, only to find that a monk has robbed him. The monk offered Jack some magic beans he stole from Roderick as collateral for Jack's horse. Back at the castle, Isabelle quarreled with her father King Brahmwell as she wanted to explore the kingdom, but he wanted her to stay and marry Roderick. Likewise, Jack's uncle scolded him for being foolish before throwing the beans on the floor and left the house.

Determined to be free, Isabelle sneaked out of the castle and seeked shelter from the rain in Jack's house. As it rains, one of the beans took root and grown into a massive beanstalk that carried the house and Isabelle into the sky as Jack fell to the ground.

Jack, Roderick, and Roderick's attendant Wicke volunteer to join the king's knights, led by Elmont and his second in-command, Crawe, and climb the beanstalk in search of Isabelle. As they climb, Roderick and Wicke cut the safety rope, intentionally killing some of the knights. At the top, they discovered the giants' realm and decided to split into two groups: one with Jack, Elmont, and Crawe, and the other including Roderick and Wicke, but not before Roderick forcibly took the remaining beans from Jack (although Jack manages to save one for himself).

Jack's group was trapped by a giant, who took everyone prisoner except Jack. Meanwhile, Roderick's group encountered two other giants; one ate Wicke, but before they could do the same to Roderick, Roderick dons the magic crown.

Jack followed the giant to their stronghold, where the two-headed giant leader, Fallon, had killed Crawe. There, Jack found the imprisoned Isabelle and Elmont. As the giants prepared to kill their remaining prisoners, Roderick walked in and enslaved the giants with the crown. He told the giants they would attack Cloister at dawn and gave them permission to eat Isabelle and Elmont. Jack rescued Isabelle and Elmont as one of the giants prepared to cook Elmont as a pig-in-a-blanket. The trio made for the beanstalk, where Jack causes the giant guarding the beanstalk to fall off the realm's edge. Seeing the giant's body, Brahmwell ordered the beanstalk cut down to avoid an invasion by the giants.

Jack and Isabelle headed down the beanstalk, while Elmont stayed to confront Roderick. Elmont killed Roderick, but Fallon took the crown before Elmont could claim it, and Elmont was forced to escape down the beanstalk. Jack, Isabelle, and Elmont all survived the fall after the beanstalk is cut down. As everyone returned home, Jack warned that the giants were using Roderick's beans to create beanstalks to descend down to Earth and attack Cloister.

The giants chased Jack, Isabelle, and Brahmwell into the castle, where Elmont filled the moat with oil and set it on fire. Fallon fell in the moat and broke into the castle from below. As the siege continued, Fallon captured Jack and Isabelle, but Jack throws the final bean down Fallon's throat, causing a beanstalk to rip apart his body. Jack took the crown and sent the giants back to their realm.

Jack and Isabelle married and told the story of the giants to their children. As time passes, the magic crown was crafted into St Edward's Crown and was secured in the Tower of London
