Jawaban:

We know from the conversations that ...

Siti’s house is far from her school but she always comes on time. She is never late because she goes to school at 6.

Lina's room is very unique. It has hings with many different colours and shapes, such as two pink chairs, a red bed, a rectangle table, a round table and a blue bookshelf. Lina never realized that her room is very colourful and has many things with different shapes.

Beny's couch is very comfortable. Edo and Udin like its colour. Beny has a very big TV.

Siti's kitchen is small and very nice. There is a cute purple dish rack. Everything in the kitchen is very well-organized and very neat.

Pembahasan

Pada soal, siswa diminta mengumpulkan informasi yang diperoleh dari percakapan.

Dialog 2 (In Lina's room)

Siti : Lina, your room is very unique. It has things with many different colours and shapes.

Lina : Yes, that’s right. It has two pink chairs, a red bed, a rectangle table...

Siti : And a round table and a blue bookshelf.

Lina : You’re right. I never realized that my room is very colourful and has many things with different shapes

Informasi yang diperoleh adalah Kamar Lina sangat unik. Kamarnya mempunyai banyak warna dan benuk yang berbeda, seperti dua kursi merah muda, sebuah tempat tidur merah, sebuah meja persegi, sebuah meja bundar, dan sebuah rak buku biru. Lina tidak menyadari bahwa kamarnya sangat berwarna warni dan mempunyai banyak benda yang berbeda bentuk.

Dialog 3 (In Beni's living room)

Udin : Beni, your couch is very comfortable.

Beni : Thank you. I like this couch, too.

Edo : And I like the colour.

Udin : Yeah, I like dark brown, too.

Edo : And you have a very big TV. It’s awesome.

Informasi yang diperoleh adalah sofa milik Beni sangat nyaman. Beni dan Edo menyukai warnanya. Beni mempunyai sebuah TV yang sangat besar.

Dialog 4 (In Siti's Kitchen)

Siti : Sorry guys, the kitchen is small. It’s not comfortable for all of us.

Lisa : It’s okay. I think it’s a very nice kitchen. Look at this purple dish rack. It’s very cute.

Edo : Yes, and everything is very well-organized. It’s a very neat kitchen.

Informasi yang diperoleh adalah Dapur Siti kecil dan sangat bagus. Ada sebuah rak piring ungu yang manis. Segala sesuatu di dapur sangat teratur dan sangat rapi.
