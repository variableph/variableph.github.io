Asking Opinion adalah menanyakan pendapat seseorang tentang hal-hal tertentu. Expression:

What do you think...?What is your opinion?What is your point of view?

Giving opinion adalah memberikan pendapat tentang hal-hal tertentu. Response:

I think ...in my Opinion...in my point of view
