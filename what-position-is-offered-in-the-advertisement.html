Jawaban:

Here are three examples of advertisements:

A television commercial for a new brand of laundry detergent:

a. The advertisement is about a new brand of laundry detergent.

b. The purpose of the advertisement is to convince consumers to buy the detergent.

c. The target consumers for this advertisement would be people who do their own laundry.

d. The product being offered to consumers is the new brand of laundry detergent.

A magazine ad for a luxury car:

a. The advertisement is about a luxury car.

b. The purpose of the advertisement is to entice consumers to buy the car.

c. The target consumers for this advertisement would be people who can afford to buy a luxury car.

d. The product being offered to consumers is the luxury car.

A social media ad for a new phone app:

a. The advertisement is about a new phone app.

b. The purpose of the advertisement is to promote the app and encourage people to download it.

c. The target consumers for this advertisement would be people who use smartphones and are interested in downloading new apps.

d. The product being offered to consumers is the new phone app.

11. In what arrangement the font size and picture shouldbe position in advertisement?​


In advertising media Fonts should be set at the most comfortable size possible to be clearly read, the most popular font types used in advertising media are Times New Roman, Palatino, Georgia, Courier, Bookman and Garamond. While on print advertising media the font size is not smaller than 10.

As for the image, the image should be in a prominent position in accordance with the theme of the advertisement. Not too crowded and interesting to look at.

Penjelasan:

Selecting the proper font in advertisement materials can make a big difference in grabbing attentions. If the fonts are too small and hard to read, many potential customers won't bother to take a look. If you make them too large, your marketing efforts might look amateurish and awkward. Use a unified set of fonts in all of your marketing messages and we will grab our prospects' attention and encourage them to buy.

The composition of the image. Whether you’ll be taking a photo yourself or sourcing on the internet to use in your design, you’ll want to make sure that it leaves a good, clear space to place your text.

19. What job is required in the advertisement ?​


Jawaban:

• Account Service

• Creative Field

• Media Field

• Ad Production

• Marketing Research

• Marketing Consultant

Penjelasan:

Account Service / Account Management

Field of work that handles the affairs of marketing advertising services (from the advertising company). The main task of this field is to find new clients (companies / advertisers who want to use advertising services) by bidding or participating in tenders / pitching advertising work.

Creative Field

Field of work that handles the design / creative design, in the form of visuals (pictures / photos) as well as text (text) advertisements. This field is also responsible for providing guidance / guidance when the creative ideas it creates will be realized / realized into advertising material. This field is also referred to as "Bina Cipta" and its work is known as a copy writer and advertising visualizer visual advertisement.

Media Field

This line of work is responsible for determining the advertising media that will be selected / used to deliver advertising messages, schedule the appearance / loading of advertisements on the selected media, and calculate the costs required to use these media. This field is also responsible for managing advertisements on selected / determined advertising media. People who handle jobs in the media field are referred to as Media Planner Media Planners and Media Buyers.

Ad Production / Execution Field

Field of work whose job is to realize creative ideas in the form of advertising material. This field handles the making of images, photographs, recording / making radio advertisements, shooting / making film advertisements for TV or cinema and making Billboard advertisements. The production sector works on advertising materials based on ideas / designs created by the Creative Field.

Marketing Research

This field is responsible for finding information about market conditions, competition, consumer tastes, responses to advertisements that have been aired, etc., through research on the public / consumers. The findings of the study will be used as consideration in planning promotional / advertising programs.

Marketing Consultant

This field provides consulting / advisory services in the field of marketing, namely how to design products, determine prices, design packaging, choose distribution areas, how to deal with competition, etc.

Sumber: Duncan, Tom.(2005) Advertising & IMC, 2nd Ed., McGraw-Hill
