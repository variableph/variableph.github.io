26. which statement is not true about the instruction​


The untrue statement about the instruction is all people have the same size rectangle to make the ring. In the instruction, "cut the flannel in a rectangle (the size depends on your finger)". Thus, the correct answer is C.

Pembahasan

Soal di atas memiliki instruksi sebagai berikut.

How to Make Flannel Rose Ring

Ingredients

Red Flannel around 10×10 cmScissorsGlue

The ways:

Cut round the flannel about 5×3 cm and make a spiral in it.Glue each side of the spiral flannel.Twist it from central until the end (look like a rose).Cut the flannel into a rectangle (the size depends on your finger).Put the rose in a rectangle and glue it.The flannel rose ring is ready to wear.

Dari pilihan jawaban di atas:

A. We must glue the rose in a rectangle to get the ring. ⇒ Benar, sesuai instruksi nomor 5.

B. The biggest the finger means the more flannel that needed. ⇒ Benar, sesuai instruksi nomor 4

C. All people have the same size of rectangle to make the ring. ⇒ Tidak benar karena tidak sesuai dengan instruksi nomor 4 "the size depends  on your finger".

D. We make a rectangle after we make a rose form. ⇒ Benar, sesuai dengan instruksi nomor 3 dan nomor 4.
