5). Apa pokok utama dari paragraf ke-2..

↪ Jawab : Yusuf meminta tetangganya untuk membantu.

Dalam paragraf ke-2 dijelaskan bahwa "Yusuf terlalu kecil untuk membantu ibunya, dan dia terlalu takut untuk berbicara kepadanya. Tapi dia berlari ke rumah tetangganya dan minta tetangganya untuk membantu ibunya. Tetangga segera memadamkan api dan membawa ibunya ke klinik".

Find the total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled.

Total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled = 15 km + 6 km

Total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled = 21 km

Find the total time taken for the whole journey.

Total time taken for the whole journey = ½ hour + ¼ hour

Total time taken for the whole journey = [tex]( \frac{2}{4} + \frac{1}{4})hour[/tex]

Total time taken for the whole journey = ¾ hour or 45 minutes.

What was the average speed for the whole journey in km/h?

First travel, she took ½ hour to travel 15 km, it means the speed is 15 km/½h or 30 km/h.Second travel, she took ¼ hour to travel 6 km, it means the speed is 6 km/¼h or 24 km/h.

[tex] = \frac{30 \: km \: per \: hour + 24 \: km \: per \: hour}{2} \\ = \frac{54 \: km \: per \: hour}{2} [/tex]

= 27 km/h
